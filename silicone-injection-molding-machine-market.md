<p><ul><li>Horizontal Silicone Injection Molding Machine (Himm)</li><li>Vertical Silicone Injection Molding Machine (Vimm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone injection molding machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Horizontal Silicone Injection Molding Machines (HIMM) and Vertical Silicone Injection Molding Machines (VIMM). HIMMs are designed for large-scale production, offering efficient material handling and a streamlined manufacturing process, ideal for producing complex silicone parts. In contrast, VIMMs are suited for smaller, intricate designs, allowing for better accessibility and stability during the molding process. Both types serve diverse applications across automotive, healthcare, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
